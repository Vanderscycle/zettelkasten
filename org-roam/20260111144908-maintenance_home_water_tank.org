#+title: Maintenance - Home - Water tank
#+date: [2026-01-11 Sun 14:49]

* How water tank maintenance
** Procedure
- prepare the area
- Shut the cold water inlet valve
- With a drain tray to collect drips, connect a hose to the bottom of the water tank that leads outside
- Open the valve, drain the tank, open a hot water tap to allow for air to escape
- Once drained, close the valve, reopen the cold water inlet, and clean the area

** Timeframe
Done yearly, and more often if the water is hard
